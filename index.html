What is Fantasy Football?

Created in 1961 Fantasy Football is a simulation of you mananging your very own NFL team. Compete with your friends and family and outperform the opposing teams players to take your championship!

What are the Effects?

Fantasy Fantasy football can drasticly change how you view your personal team verses your home city team. Fantasy can change your bias, change your viewing standpoint, and maybe change your life?

Before this great invention the average fan waits for their city team to compete with others once every sunday. Fantasy Football revolutionized this boring NFL Lifestyle with fans tuning in to many games focused on their very own players."

How did this Start?

In the early times of fantasy football everything had to be done pen and paper which is niether time or cost effective. The internet explosion boomed Fantasy Footba for the better and online platofrms such as Yahoo and ESPN allowed for global participation.

Fantasy Football started of as a quick niche hobby that turns into a cultural phenomenon. Fantasy football created fan engagement with many different teams, and even had to put them in a position to go against their own. It gives fans importance and a personal connection towards the NFL, giving them a sense of ownership.
